Who We Are

An Austin music collective working in deep house, disco, and soul.

Floppy Disko started in 2023 as a rotating roster of DJs, producers, and visual artists in Austin, Texas. The collective is built around accessibility and inclusivity — making space for music that brings people together rather than gatekeeping it.

We work across vinyl and digital. We play warehouses, clubs, festivals, and friends' backyards. We care about the room as much as the lineup.

The Crew

Six DJs, two producers, and two visual designers. Each member brings a different lane into the mix — disco, deep house, soul, edits, original production, photography, design. The work is collective; the credits are individual.

The Sound

Disco and deep house at the center, with the freedom to drift into soul, boogie, Italo, jazz-funk, and house edges. We program for energy, not genre purity.

The Ethos

Embracing unity through music. Driven by the groove. Built around the idea that dancing is a public good and the dance floor is for everyone.
